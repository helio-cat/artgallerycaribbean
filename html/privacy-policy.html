Information that is gathered from visitors

In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving your details such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, referring page, the pages of our site you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages and other statistics.

Cookies are small digital signature files that are stored by your web browser and used to remember your preferences when visiting the website.

How the Information is used
